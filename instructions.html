Sports Quiz

A quiz about everything in Sports

The quiz contains 20 questions.
You will get the option to choose between 4 alternatives to the question asked.
When you have chosen your answer, click on "Answer" to get the result.
If the answer is Correct / Incorrect the page & answer will go "GREEN" / "RED".
You get 2 points for each questions answered. A totoal score of 40 points to complete game.
To get back to the Sports quiz click on the "ARROW".
To play game "AGAIN", refresh browers.
